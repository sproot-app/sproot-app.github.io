Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-05 Operator: the Sproot developer (individual) sproot.app@gmail.com

1. Our principle

Sproot ("the App") is designed not to collect personal information. No account is required, and the App contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking.

2. Where your data lives

Word books, cards, study history, and settings are stored only on your device and are never sent to the operator's servers. Whether they are included in device backups (e.g., iCloud) follows your iOS settings.

3. Network access

The App communicates externally only in the following cases:

  • AI model download — the on-device AI model file is fetched from a distribution server. No personal information is transmitted.
  • "Fact-check with Wikipedia" (opt-in) — only when you turn this on, the topic search term you enter is sent to Wikipedia (Wikimedia Foundation) during card generation. Your study history, word-book contents, and device information are never sent. You can turn it off anytime in Settings.
  • Cloud AI (when offered; paid plan) — only if you choose to use it, the topic or text read from your photo is sent for generation, with separate consent on first use.

4. Camera & photos

The camera and photo library are used solely to read notes and create cards. Text recognition happens on-device; images are never uploaded.

5. Notifications

Study reminders use local notifications only.

6. Third parties

The operator does not share personal information with third parties (none is collected in the first place).

7. Children's privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and collects no personal information from children.

8. Changes

Any changes to this policy will be announced on this page.
